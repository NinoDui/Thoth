Last year, we thoroughly studied and implemented the guiding principles from the 20th CPC National Congress and the Second Plenary Session of the 20th CPC Central Committee and implemented the decisions and plans of the Party Central Committee. On that basis, we mainly carried out the following work:

1. We intensified macro regulation and promoted a steady economic recovery. 2. We prioritized the development of a modernized industrial system and accelerated the development of new quality productive forces. 3. We deepened reform and opening up and worked to stimulate the vitality of various market entities. 4. We promoted a new type of urbanization and rural revitalization and worked to promote coordinated development between urban and rural areas and between regions. 5. We worked to ensure and improve people’s wellbeing and focused on resolving the most pressing problems of the people. 6. We worked to improve the environment and accelerated the transition to a green and low-carbon development model. 7. We worked to prevent and defuse risks and ensured the overall stability of the economy and society....
